# Member/Sponsor/Partner Spotlight Generator

Generate a spotlight post featuring a SAME SF member, sponsor, or partner organization.

## Before Generating

1. **Read these files**:
   - `data/brand-voice.md` — warm, specific, celebratory tone
   - `data/exclusion-list.md` — banned words/phrases
   - `data/content-themes.md` — #MemberMonday and Sponsor Recognition formats

## Input Required

Run this checklist. Ask for any missing items:
- **Who**: Name, title, credentials (PE, PMP, EIT, F.SAME, etc.)
- **Organization**: Company or agency
- **Spotlight type**: Member / Sponsor / Partner
- **SAME SF connection**: How they're involved (board, volunteer, sponsor, YP, past president)
- **Story/quote**: Personal connection to SAME, memorable moment, why they're involved
- **Achievement**: Recent award, project, promotion, or contribution
- **Photo**: Available? Description for graphic if not
- **Audience**: Members / Sponsors / DoD / Students / General
- **Also need sponsor deck blurb?** (for sponsor spotlights — shorter mission-aligned blurb for decks/follow-ups)

**Output rule**: Always produce Version A (short) + Version B (full) + 1 alt opening. For sponsor spotlights, also produce a **Deck Blurb** (2-3 sentences for sponsor decks and follow-up emails).

## Spotlight Types

### Member Spotlight (#MemberMonday)
```
[Series tag] #MemberMonday [or #MemberAppreciation #ThrowbackThursday]

Meet [Full Name, Credentials], [Title] at [Organization] and [SAME SF role].

[2-3 sentences: their SAME SF journey — how they got involved, what they've contributed]

[1-2 sentences: personal quote or specific story that humanizes them]

[Closing: what they represent for the community]

#SAMESF #AECCommunity [+ relevant hashtags]
```
- Tag their personal LinkedIn profile and company page
- 200-300 words
- Include a quote if available

### Sponsor Spotlight
```
[Gratitude opening — not generic, tie to specific impact]

[2-3 sentences: what this sponsor has enabled (scholarships, events, programs)]

[Specific numbers if available: years of support, dollars contributed, students impacted]

[Forward-looking close: continued partnership]

#SAMESF #AECCommunity [+ sponsor's typical hashtags]
```
- Tag the company page
- Mention sponsor tier if relevant (Diamond, Platinum, Gold, Silver)
- 150-250 words

### Partner Spotlight
```
[Partnership introduction — how SAME SF and this partner work together]

[Specific collaboration: joint event, scholarship program, site visit hosting]

[Impact: what this partnership has produced]

[Appreciation close]

#SAMESF [+ partner hashtags]
```
- Tag the partner organization
- 150-250 words

## Writing Tips

- Lead with the person/org, not with SAME SF
- Use their actual words if you have a quote
- Be specific: "served as Post President from 2018-2020" not "has been a leader"
- Connect their story to the broader SAME SF mission
- Don't make every spotlight sound the same — vary the opening hook
