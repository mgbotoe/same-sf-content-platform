### Version B (Full — 260 words)

We're building our 2026 meeting calendar, and we want your expertise on it.

SAME San Francisco Post hosts monthly in-person meetings where members connect, learn, and share. One of the best parts of these meetings? Hearing from our own members about the work they do, the projects they lead, and the lessons they've picked up along the way.

We're putting out a call: if you have a presentation, a talk, a case study, or a topic you'd be willing to share at an upcoming meeting, we'd love to hear from you.

What we're looking for:
- A/E/C project case studies (military, federal, or civilian)
- Federal contracting process, tips, or lessons learned
- New technology, tools, or methods in engineering and construction
- Leadership, mentorship, or career development topics
- STEM outreach and education approaches
- Military engineering or infrastructure projects

Format is flexible. It can be a 20-minute talk, a 45-minute deep dive, or a panel discussion. Slides, demos, or just a conversation. Whatever works for you and the topic.

Our members include military engineers, government project managers, A/E/C firm leaders, and students. The one thing they all have in common? They want to learn from people doing the work.

If you've given a talk at a conference, a company meeting, or a professional event and think it would fit, let us know.

Email samesanfrancisco@gmail.com with your topic and a brief description. We'll take it from there.

#SAMESF #AECCommunity #MilitaryEngineers #ProfessionalDevelopment #ServiceAndCommunity
