SPOTLIGHT EVENT

USCG Industry Day & Open House

Co-hosted by SAME San Francisco Post and U.S. Coast Guard Civil Engineering Unit Oakland

On March 12, we're bringing the A/E/C community inside Coast Guard Civil Engineering Unit Oakland for an afternoon of briefings, networking, and direct conversation with the people who plan and execute USCG construction and facilities projects.

What you'll get:

•  A briefing on CEUO's structure and contracting process
•  Panel discussion on upcoming opportunities
•  Networking with branch chiefs and contracting personnel
•  Breakout sessions by opportunity area
Date: Thursday, March 12, 2026
Time: 2:00 PM – 5:00 PM
Location: Civil Engineering Unit Oakland
Cost: FREE
After: No-host social

Open to general contractors, specialty subcontractors, A/E firms, small businesses, and anyone interested in federal contracting with the Coast Guard.

STEM & SCHOLARSHIPS

Investing in the Next Generation

Our scholarship program continues to grow, funding future engineers and leaders across the Bay Area. Through partnerships with USF and the Jonathan Cartwright Endowment, we're helping bridge the gap between academic potential and professional opportunity.

Whether it's mentoring, sponsoring a student, or contributing to the endowment, there's a role for everyone in building the next generation of A/E/C leaders.

Did you know? Military, government, and student SAME members get FREE registration to JETC (Joint Engineer Training Conference) — the premier annual event for the military engineering community.

COMMUNITY

It's National Engineers Week!

National Engineers Week (February 16–22) just wrapped up, and we wanted to take a moment to recognize the engineers who design, build, and protect the infrastructure we all depend on.

At SAME SF, we connect military, government, and private-sector engineers year-round. From site visits and technical forums to STEM scholarships and mentorship, our work lives at the intersection of engineering and national service.

To every engineer in our network: this week is yours. And if you know someone who should be part of our community, now's a great time to invite them.
