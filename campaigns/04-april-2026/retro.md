# Retro — Geospatial Tools for A/E/C Panel (April 16, 2026)

## What we promised
3-post LinkedIn sequence driving registration to a paid panel event at the Chicken Pie Shop, Walnut Creek. Featuring Trimble. Audience: A/E/C professionals, public agency asset managers. Registration via MemberPlanet.

## What actually happened
Post 1 (March 24): Published as a LinkedIn Event page — correct call for a ticketed event with logistics.
Post 2 (March 31): Status was "Drafting" at event time — unclear if it shipped on schedule.
Post 3 (April 14 last call): Status was "Drafting" at event time — unclear if it shipped.

Event ran April 16. Recap published May 5 (see `05-may-2026/05-05-geospatial-recap.md`).

**What we don't know:**
- Final attendance vs. registration count

## What we change next time
1. **Mark posts as "Scheduled" or "Posted" explicitly** — "Drafting" status when the event date passes is a red flag. The index should catch this.
2. **LinkedIn Event vs. standard post** — LinkedIn Event format was the right call for Post 1. Posts 2 and 3 should share the Event, not create new posts. Confirm this is in the event-campaign skill template.
3. **Follow up with sponsors within one week** — Trimble sponsored and presented. A thank-you post or DM within 7 days keeps the relationship warm for future events.
4. **Registration link in first comment** worked well for this format. Keep it.
5. **Pricing in the post** — including specific dollar amounts (Government: $40, Member: $50) in Posts 1 and 2 is strong. Don't hide pricing behind a link.
