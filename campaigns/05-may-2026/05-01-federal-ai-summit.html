Introducing the Federal AI Summit: AI for A/E/C

The SAME San Francisco Post is proud to announce the Federal AI Summit: AI for A/E/C. A first-of-its-kind event coming to Stanford University on September 10, 2026, hosted in partnership with Stanford's Center for Integrated Facility Engineering (CIFE).

AI is reshaping the built environment faster than most organizations are ready for. This summit exists to change that — bringing together leaders from federal agencies, AEC, academia, technology, and venture capital for a full day of substantive conversation, practical insights, and cross-sector connection.

What to expect:

•  Keynote speakers and panels on AI's impact across federal infrastructure
•  Cross-sector networking with peers from government, industry, and academia
•  Actionable takeaways you can bring back to your organization

Whether you're navigating AI adoption, building technology for the built environment, or setting policy that will shape both — this is the room to be in.

📅 Date: Thursday, September 10, 2026

📍 Location: Stanford University, Stanford, CA

🎫 Early Bird: $250 for SAME members (available for a limited time)

We look forward to seeing you at Stanford.
