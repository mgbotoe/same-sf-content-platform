## Version B (Full — 140 words)

Today we remember.

Memorial Day exists for one reason: to honor the American service members who gave their lives defending this country. Not a long weekend. Not a sales event. A day of remembrance for lives cut short in service to something larger than themselves.

At SAME San Francisco Post, our community includes veterans, active-duty members, and military families who carry this day differently than most. Some of our members served beside people whose names are now on headstones at national cemeteries, on walls of remembrance, on plaques at installations across the country.

We honor them not with words alone, but by continuing the mission they believed in: strengthening the connection between military service and the profession of engineering.

Their sacrifice is not forgotten.

We remember.

#SAMESF #MemorialDay #NeverForget
