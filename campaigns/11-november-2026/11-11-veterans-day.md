## Version B (Full — 355 words)

They designed airfields under deadline. They built bases in terrain no one else would touch. They managed construction programs spanning entire theaters of operation. And when their service ended, they brought every one of those skills home.

Today is Veterans Day, and we honor every American who has served in uniform. 🇺🇸

Veterans Day is not Memorial Day. Memorial Day honors those who gave their lives. Veterans Day honors the living, the men and women who raised their right hand, served their country, and returned to civilian life carrying experience, perspective, and purpose that few other paths provide.

At SAME San Francisco Post, we see that every day. Our membership includes veterans from every branch of service: Army, Navy, Air Force, Marine Corps, Coast Guard, and Space Force. They are project engineers at firms across the Bay Area. They are program managers overseeing federal construction. They are small business owners, senior leaders, and young professionals building the next phase of their careers.

The common thread is this: they served, and they kept serving. Not in uniform, but through the work of designing, building, and maintaining the infrastructure that supports our military and our communities.

SAME, the Society of American Military Engineers, was founded in 1920 with the purpose of connecting military and civilian engineers for national security. More than a century later, that purpose holds. And at the heart of it are veteran professionals who understand both sides of that partnership.

Our Post's events, from the Holiday Gala to our monthly programs to our scholarship fundraisers, are shaped by veterans who volunteer their time and bring their networks to the table. The engineering and construction industry is stronger because of them.

To every veteran: thank you for your service, and thank you for the work you continue to do in our community.

If you're a veteran in the A/E/C industry, or if you work alongside veterans and want to strengthen that connection, SAME SF is the place. Join us. 🇺🇸

Who's a veteran in your network? Tag them below and say thank you.
