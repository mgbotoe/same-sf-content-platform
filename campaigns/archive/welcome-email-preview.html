Welcome to SAME San Francisco!

We're glad you're here.

The SAME San Francisco Post connects military, government, and A/E/C industry professionals across the Bay Area. Whether you're a longtime SAME member or just learning about us, you've joined a community that's been strengthening national security infrastructure and supporting the next generation of engineers for decades.
 

A Message from Our President

For Students & Young Professionals

Whether you're in school or early in your career, SAME has programs designed to help you grow, connect, and lead.

SAME STEM/Engineering Camps — week-long experiences at military bases
Student Membership — join SAME as a student
Leader Development Programs

Our SF Post YP co-chairs LTJG Ben Acebo (USCG) and Isabel Andaya (HDR Inc.) are great points of contact for young professionals looking to get involved.

The SF Post awards scholarships through the Nicholas Fund, Bentley Fund, and the Porterville Military Academy / Oakland Military Institute endowments.
