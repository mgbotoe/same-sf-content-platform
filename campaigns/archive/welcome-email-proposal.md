# Welcome Email Proposal — BOD Email Draft

## Email to Board of Directors

**To:** SAME SF Board of Directors
**From:** Madina Gbotoe
**Subject:** Proposal: Automated Welcome Email for New Subscribers

---

Hi everyone,

I'd like to propose adding an automated welcome email for new subscribers to our Mailchimp list. Right now, when someone subscribes, they don't hear from us until the next newsletter goes out — which could be weeks later. That's a missed opportunity to make a great first impression.

**Why it matters:**
- Welcome emails have an average 50% open rate — higher than any other email type
- Sets expectations so subscribers know what they're getting (and are less likely to unsubscribe later)
- Immediately connects new members to upcoming events and ways to get involved
- Our Mailchimp Free plan includes one automation, so there's no additional cost

Below is a draft of what the welcome email would look like. I'd love your feedback before we set it up.

---

## Proposed Welcome Email

**Subject line:** Welcome to the SAME San Francisco Post
**Preview text:** Here's what to expect and how to get involved.

---

### Email Body

**Welcome to SAME San Francisco!**

We're glad you're here.

The SAME San Francisco Post connects military, government, and A/E/C industry professionals across the Bay Area. Whether you're a longtime SAME member or just learning about us, you've joined a community that's been strengthening national security infrastructure and supporting the next generation of engineers for decades.

**What you'll hear from us:**

- Monthly newsletters with post updates, member spotlights, and industry news
- Event invitations — site visits, happy hours, and networking events
- Military observances honoring the branches that make our mission possible
- Annual highlights: our Golf Tournament (July) and Holiday Gala (December)

We keep it to 1-2 emails per week at most, and usually less. No spam, no clutter.

**Get involved:**

- **Attend an event** — Check our upcoming events at [Events Page link]
- **Follow us on LinkedIn** — [LinkedIn page link] for posts, photos, and community updates
- **Volunteer** — We're always looking for people to help with events, outreach, and STEM programs. Just reply to this email if you're interested.

**Questions?** Reply to this email anytime — a real person reads every message.

Welcome aboard,
The SAME San Francisco Post Team

---

## Notes for BOD Review

1. **Tone**: Warm and welcoming, consistent with our brand voice — not corporate, not salesy
2. **Frequency**: Sends once, automatically, only to new subscribers at the moment they join
3. **Links to customize**: Events page URL, LinkedIn page URL
4. **Optional additions the board may want**:
   - Photo of a recent event (Gala, site visit) to give visual context
   - Names of current board leadership as a personal touch
   - Link to a membership benefits page or PDF
   - Scholarship/STEM impact stat ("Last year we awarded $X in scholarships")
5. **Next steps if approved**: I'll build it in Mailchimp using our newsletter template style and activate the automation

Please let me know your thoughts or any changes you'd like to see. Happy to adjust before we go live.

Best,
Madina
