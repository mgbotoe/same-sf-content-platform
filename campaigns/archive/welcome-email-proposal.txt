Welcome to SAME San Francisco!

We're glad you're here.

The SAME San Francisco Post connects military, government, and A/E/C industry professionals across the Bay Area. Whether you're a longtime SAME member or just learning about us, you've joined a community that's been strengthening national security infrastructure and supporting the next generation of engineers for decades.


A Message from Our President
-----------------------------

"Welcome to the SAME San Francisco Post. Whether you're joining us from the military, government, or industry side, you're now part of a community that's been making a difference in the Bay Area for decades. I look forward to meeting you at an upcoming event."

   President, SAME San Francisco Post
   US Coast Guard - CEU Oakland


Here's what to expect:

- Monthly newsletters with post updates, member spotlights, and industry news
- Event invitations -- site visits, happy hours, and networking events
