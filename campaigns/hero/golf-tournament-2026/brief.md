## 1. Objective
Drive registration and sponsorship for the 2026 SAME SF Golf Tournament. Primary action: get members and sponsors to register. Secondary action: recruit new sponsors (Diamond, Platinum, event-level).

## 2. Audience Focus

| Audience | Priority | What they need |

| Past attendees | Primary | "It's back, it's in July" — date change awareness |
