### Body

**Co-hosted by SAME San Francisco Post and U.S. Coast Guard Civil Engineering Unit Oakland**

On March 12, we're bringing the A/E/C community inside Coast Guard Civil Engineering Unit Oakland for an afternoon of briefings, networking, and direct conversation with the people who plan and execute USCG construction projects.

**What you'll get:**
- A briefing on CEUO's structure and how to work with them
- Panel discussion on upcoming contracting opportunities
- Networking with CEUO branch chiefs and contracting personnel
- Breakout sessions by opportunity area
- One-on-one meetings with USCG contracting officers (advance signup)

**Event Details:**
- Date: Thursday, March 12, 2026
- Time: 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
- Location: Civil Engineering Unit Oakland
- Cost: FREE
- After: No-host social (location TBD)

This event is open to general contractors, specialty subcontractors, A/E firms, small businesses, and anyone interested in federal contracting with the Coast Guard.
