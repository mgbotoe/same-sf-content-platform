# Retro — USCG CEU Oakland Industry Day (March 12, 2026)

## What we promised
Connect the A/E/C community with USCG Civil Engineering Unit Oakland contracting leadership. Two LinkedIn posts (Feb 25 announcement, March 10 last call) plus email and a flyer. Goal: get qualified contractors and engineers into the room.

## What actually happened
Event ran March 12. Both LinkedIn posts shipped. Flyer and email content were produced.

**What we don't know (no data captured):**
- Attendance count and how it compared to capacity
- LinkedIn engagement on both posts
- Whether the email drove registrations
- Whether 1-on-1 meeting slots filled up

**Process failure to flag:** The registration link was marked [TBD] in both LinkedIn posts and was never filled in before publishing. Both posts went out with a placeholder. This is a gap in the pre-publish checklist — any post with a [TBD] field should be blocked until the field is live.

## What we change next time
1. **Registration link is a hard dependency** — no announcement post ships without a live registration URL. Add this as a checklist item in the event-campaign skill.
2. **Capture attendance** — ask LT Duncan Clark for headcount and any feedback from CEUO within one week of the event. Log it here.
3. **Pull LinkedIn metrics** at the 7-day mark for each post. Even basic (impressions, comments) gives a baseline for future co-hosted government events.
4. **Email metrics** — check open rate and click rate in Mailchimp within 48 hours of send.
