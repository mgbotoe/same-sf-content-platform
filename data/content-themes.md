# SAME SF Content Themes & Recurring Series

## Content Pillars

### 1. Community & Connection
Posts about events, networking, member gatherings, and the people who make SAME SF work.
- Event promotions and recaps
- Member appreciation
- Happy hours and casual meetups
- Cross-post collaboration (Sacramento Post, national SAME)

### 2. Service & Honor
Military observances, veteran support, community resources during crises.
- Military birthday posts
- Memorial/remembrance posts
- Community resource sharing (e.g., government shutdown support)
- Veteran career support

### 3. Education & Future
STEM scholarships, student spotlights, mentorship, academic partnerships.
- Scholarship announcements and recaps
- USF partnership highlights
- Student achievement features
- Military academy support (Porterville, Oakland Military Institute)

### 4. Industry & Infrastructure
Site visits, technical content, A/E/C industry highlights, public-private partnerships.
- Site visit announcements and recaps
- Infrastructure project features
- Technical sessions and panels
- SAME National program highlights

### 5. Legacy & History
Throwback content, Post history, traditions, "Then & Now" comparisons.
- #ThrowbackThursday posts
- Archival photo features
- "Then & Now" carousels
- Legacy member stories

## Recurring Series

### #MemberMonday
- **Frequency**: 1-2x per month
- **Format**: Photo + 200-word profile
- **Content**: Name, role, SAME SF involvement, quote, personal connection to mission
- **Best practice**: Tag the person's company page and personal profile

### #ThrowbackThursday
- **Frequency**: 1-2x per month (more during campaign ramp-ups)
- **Format**: Archival photo + storytelling narrative
- **Content**: Historical event, project, or tradition connected to a current initiative
- **Best practice**: Use "Then & Now" carousel format for higher engagement

### Volunteer Spotlight
- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Format**: Photo + recognition post
- **Content**: Volunteer name, what they contributed, impact
- **Best practice**: Schedule during Member Appreciation Month (September)

### Sponsor Recognition
- **Frequency**: As needed (heavy during Golf Tournament + Gala seasons)
- **Format**: Company logo graphic + gratitude post
- **Content**: Sponsor name, tier, specific impact of their support
- **Best practice**: Tag company page, mention specific programs funded

## Content Mix (Monthly Target)

**Hard cap: 12 posts/month maximum.** Beyond 12, engagement drops 45% and impressions drop 44% (confirmed by both industry research and SAME SF's own data).

| Type | Posts/Month | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Event promotion | **2-3** | Quality over quantity; zero promo posts cracked our top 10 |
| Military observances | 1-3 | Per calendar, never skip Veterans Day/Memorial Day |
| **Recaps & spotlights** | **3-4** | **Highest ROI content type** (every top-10 post was a recap or spotlight) |
| Scholarship/STEM | 1-2 | Peaks around Gala season |
| Throwback Thursday | 2-3 | Strong filler content, builds Post identity |
| Industry/infrastructure | 1-2 | Site visit content, SAME National shares |
| Community resources | As needed | Responsive to current events |

**Content mix ratio (from research):**
- 60% educational/value content (recaps, spotlights, throwbacks, STEM)
- 25% thought leadership/community (member stories, industry insights)
- 15% promotional (event registration, CTAs)

**Never post 2 promotional posts back-to-back.** Interleave with value content.

## Content Repurposing Rules

### Cross-Channel Conversions
- **Newsletter Spotlight Event** → 2-3 LinkedIn posts (announcement, reminder, recap)
- **Newsletter Recap** → LinkedIn post, quote cards, photo carousel
- **Newsletter Observance** → Day-of LinkedIn post
- **High-performing LinkedIn** → Quote card, newsletter mention
- **Event photos** → Multiple posts spaced over 1-2 weeks

### Timing Rules
- **Never post** the same content on the same day across channels
- **Wait 2-4 weeks** between LinkedIn reposts of similar content
- **Different angles = new content** (e.g., same event but sponsor-focused vs. attendee-focused)

### Newsletter → LinkedIn Conversion
1. Announcement post (2 weeks before event)
2. Value proposition post (1 week before)
3. Final push (day-of or day-before)

### Evergreen Content Bank
Keep a running list of reusable content: throwbacks, member quotes, program explainers, testimonials. These fill gaps when no campaign is running.
